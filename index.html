01. Personal Loan

A personal loan (also known as a consumer loan) describes any situation in which an individual borrows money for personal need, including paying off debt, financing a large purchase such as a vehicle or a boat, or covering the cost of a major expense like a wedding or a home renovation.

Personal loans typically have lower interest rates compared to credit cards, especially if you have a high credit score. If you don't have good credit, you may end up paying a much higher interest rate. You can apply Personal Loan with us and get to know your eligibility and enjoy the benefits

02. Auto Loan

An Auto Loan is taken by borrowers to purchase a new or used vehicle. Auto loans are secured loans where the vehicle itself is used as a collateral. It is offered by lenders for new cars, used cars and top up loans

You can apply for a Vehicle Loan with us and enjoy various benefits like high-value loans at attractive interest rates, flexible repayment terms and affordable EMIs. If you have a high credit score, you may qualify for a lower interest rate, saving you money over time. Your credit score helps determine a required down payment and the initial loan amount you’re eligible to borrow.

03. Home Loan

A home loan is a secured loan that is obtained to purchase a property by offering it as collateral. Home loans offer high value funding at economical interest rates and for long tenures.

Loan eligibility refers to how much money you’ll get to buy a house and whether or not you’re eligible for a loan. To determine your home loan eligibility, certain factors must be considered, such as the applicant’s age, income, type of employment and property, credit score, and many others. You can apply Home loan with us and get to know your eligibility and enjoy the benefits
